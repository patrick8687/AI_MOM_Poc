`prompts.py` — Purpose

- Purpose: Stores prompt templates used by the generator.
- Key symbols: `MOM_PROMPT` — a formatted string where the transcript is inserted before sending to the LLM.
- Where to edit: change tone, sections, or rules in `MOM_PROMPT` to tweak MoM output.
- Notes: Keep prompts concise and include formatting instructions (Markdown, bullets, sections) for predictable output.
